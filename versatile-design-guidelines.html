Design Principles

Four values guiding every design decision under the Versatile IT Solutions brand.

⚡
Clarity First
Every layout communicates value immediately. Visual hierarchy guides the eye: awareness → trust → action. Users never wonder what to do next.
🎯
Premium Precision
Every pixel is intentional. Spacing follows an 8px grid. Shadows are layered systematically. Typography uses exact weights, sizes, and tracking.
🌐
Global Trust
Across 4 continents, the brand must feel enterprise-grade and credible. Deep blue tones signal authority; clean white space signals expertise.
🤖
AI-Forward Identity
ML & AI lead the narrative. Data visualisations, gradient accents, and futuristic spatial compositions signal technological sophistication.

Do's & Don'ts

Brand guardrails to maintain consistency and premium quality across all design touchpoints.

✓ Do
✓Use DM Sans exclusively at all weights (300–700)
✓Apply −0.03em letter-spacing on all headings H1–H4
✓Use the purple gradient for the single highest-priority CTA
✓Keep heading colours in the blue family (#14358A / #006FBA / #03133D)
✓Always pair cards with the blue-tinted shadow system
✓Float illustrations with the 6s ease-in-out keyframe
✓Use 8px grid multiples for all spacing values
✓Place icon badges in the blue→darkest-blue gradient only
✕ Don't
✗Never use Inter, Roboto, Arial, or system-ui
✗Never use the purple gradient for secondary or tertiary actions
✗Never stretch the hero gradient beyond 84° angle
✗Never use pure #000 for text — use #03133D or #1B2559
✗Never remove shadows from cards, even on white backgrounds
✗Never use border-radius above 16px on cards or buttons
✗Never place 3+ CTAs side-by-side without clear hierarchy
✗Never use the error colour (#EF4444) for decorative purposes
