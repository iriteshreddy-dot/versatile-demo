Who We Are

Innovation Partners for the Digital Age

We are Versatile IT Solutions (Versatile Mobitech Pvt. Ltd.) — an end-to-end digital transformation partner headquartered in Hyderabad, India with offices across 4 continents.

For the last 12+ years, we've sky-rocketed the growth of our partners and customers through innovation in strategy and technology. We believe in creating compounding value through precision, helping clients cause a phase-shift across their industry.

With 180+ clients globally and 300+ projects delivered, our endless curiosity spans UI/UX, Web & Mobile Development, Digital Marketing, ML & AI, IoT, Enterprise Solutions, and IT Consulting.

Meet Our Founder

Srinivas Mogilipala

Founder & CEO — Versatile Mobitech Pvt. Ltd.

Srinivas founded Versatile IT Solutions in 2012 with a team of 5 engineers and a vision to become a one-stop digital transformation partner for businesses worldwide. Under his leadership, the company has grown from a small Hyderabad startup to a global operation spanning 4 countries with 180+ clients.

His philosophy of creating compounding value through precision and innovation has driven the delivery of 300+ successful projects across FinTech, Healthcare, E-commerce, Government, and 13+ other industry verticals. Today, Srinivas continues to lead the company's expansion into AI/ML services and global markets.
